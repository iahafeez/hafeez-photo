Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Hafeez Photo (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how it’s used.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include your name, email address, event type, and any details you share in your message. We do not use tracking cookies, third-party advertising trackers, or sell any information to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry, provide quotes, and coordinate photography services. We do not add you to a marketing list or share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed through Netlify Forms, a third-party service that securely handles form submissions and delivers them to us. Netlify’s own privacy practices apply to how that data is transmitted and stored on their end.

Photography Services

This policy covers information collected through this website only. Photography services, image usage rights, and delivery are governed separately by the terms agreed upon at the time of booking.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and, if applicable, fulfill a booking.

Your Rights

If you’d like your information removed from our records, email us at ibrahim@hafeezphoto.com and we’ll take care of it.
