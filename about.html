About

Ibrahim Abdelhafeez

Corporate Event & Headshot Photographer

With over 15 years of photography experience spanning weddings, engagements, family, and commercial work, Ibrahim made the shift in 2025 to focus on what he loves most: capturing the energy of corporate events and helping professionals put their best face forward with headshots that actually look like them, just more polished.

He has covered major expos including Winter FancyFaire in San Diego and Natural Products Expo West in Anaheim, photographing dedicated brand sections and exhibitor activations across both events. He also serves as official photographer for a large annual 3-day convention, where he leads a team capturing keynotes and parallel sessions running all day, every day, a role he has held for the past 8 years.

That range, from fast-paced expo floors to a multi-day convention with back-to-back sessions, shaped how he works: fast, unobtrusive, and always thinking about how the final images will actually be used, whether on a LinkedIn profile, in a recap deck, or in next year’s sponsorship pitch.

Based in Southern California and available nationally, Ibrahim brings a full on-site studio setup, same-day highlight delivery, and a genuinely calm presence to fast-moving event floors, because a great photographer should make the day easier, not add to the chaos.
